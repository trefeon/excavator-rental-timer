Relay Murah MVP Wiring + TM1637 Display

ESP32 BLE timer + 1x18650 + relay 3V/3.3V + 4-digit countdown display + mainan excavator RC bawaan

Relay

Use 3V/3.3V coil, NO contact.

Contact rating minimal 3A, better 5A.

Driver

GPIO must drive transistor, not coil direct.

Flyback diode required.

Safety

Boot and reset must keep relay OFF.

No auto-resume after battery swap.
